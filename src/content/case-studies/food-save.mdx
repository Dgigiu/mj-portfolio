## The problem

Commercial kitchens throw away a quarter of what they buy. Most of that loss is invisible because no one has time to track it during a shift. Food Save gave kitchens a fast way to log waste, see patterns, and act on them.

## What I owned

The kitchen-side capture app, the operator dashboard, and the reporting view chefs and managers use in weekly huddles. I worked closely with chefs in their kitchens to make sure the capture flow took under ten seconds per entry.

## Key decisions

- **Speed first, accuracy second.** A rough log entered today is more valuable than a perfect one entered next week.
- **Photos as a fallback.** When typing was slow, a photo plus a weight was good enough.
- **One number on the home screen.** Cost of waste this week. Everything else is one tap away.

## Outcome

Rolled out across hotel and restaurant groups in Europe. Operators reported double-digit reductions in food waste within the first three months of use.
